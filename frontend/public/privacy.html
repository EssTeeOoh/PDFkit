Privacy Policy

PDFKit is built as a local-first document toolkit. In normal use, the app processes files through your own browser and backend, and it is designed to avoid third-party document handling services.

What we collect

  • Basic app telemetry such as frontend error events and tool usage signals.
  • Backend request logs and health checks used to keep the service stable.
  • Files you choose to upload or process through the app.

How we use it

  • To run the requested PDF action and return the result.
  • To diagnose failures and improve the app experience.
  • To keep the service secure and reliable.

Your responsibility

Only upload files you are allowed to process.
